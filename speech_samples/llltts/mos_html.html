Audio Quality Evaluation

Please rate the audio quality of the following sound clips using these scales, "Excellent", "Good", 'Fair", "Poor", and "Bad".

  • You may listen to a sound more than once if needed; just click the play button to replay
  • Your responses will be manually screened to ensure that you made a serious effort to rate the recordings. If the results are determined to be guessed or contain inconsistent answers, then the task may be rejected and may not be paid
  • Please perform these tasks in a quiet place using a pair of good headphones. It is recommended that you set the volume a bit higher than normal for these tasks
  • If you are having trouble viewing the sound clips, please try to view the test in Google Chrome
  • Please make sure that you've finished all the questions before submitting
